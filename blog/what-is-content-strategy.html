What Is Content Strategy?

You may have great developers and designers, but without a content strategist you might be throwing away your money.

I've seen this too many times. Organizations spend a lot of money trying to build digital products only to deliver something that falls far behind their expectations. No matter how hard they try, they cannot seem to deliver a product that achieves the right level of quality. The reason, surprisingly, is not poor code.
Instead, the problem lies on the domain of design. Most companies approach design as a matter exclusively of interfaces. Although this matters a lot, it does not take into consideration the one element that hides in plain sight: words.
