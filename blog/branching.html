Branching

“Branching means you diverge from the main line of development and continue to do work without messing with that main line.”

- About git branching from the book ‘Pro Git’

Sometimes I wish certain things in life resembled developing software. In particular, I like the whole idea of how Git works: to be able to diverge from one’s current path, without having to discard it, and be able to come back to edit it. One can only dream of something like that.

However, as scary as it might sound, it is also exciting to know that every decision we take in our lives away from the screen has consequences, and that taking a certain path closes another. Otherwise, we would be easily overwhelmed by the number of forks and paths.

Allowing ourselves to explore new paths can seem scary at first, but it is also what makes life interesting. It grants us the responsibility of choosing.
