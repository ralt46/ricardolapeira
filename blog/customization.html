When Is Too Much Customization Too Much?

Yes, customization can be lots of fun and can really make something seem much more intimate. But when is it too much?

Recently, I feel the weight of customization. Making things feel like my own just takes a lot of time. It’s almost like an additional task that requires my attention every now and then. Don’t even get my started about the process of learning how to customize something.

This has got me thinking that things should not be so customizable as they sometimes are. In some cases, they shouldn’t even be customizable at all. If something is truly great, it should be fine just as it is.
